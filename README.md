## What I work on

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### Predictive modeling
Building production-grade classifiers and regressors on structured data. Feature engineering, hyperparameter tuning, and model selection with XGBoost, LightGBM, and ensemble methods. Every model ships with SHAP explainability.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### Customer analytics
Churn prediction, propensity scoring, and lifetime value modeling for telecom and subscription businesses. Calibrated probabilities, decile analysis, and cost-benefit frameworks that translate model output into dollar impact.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### Time series and forecasting
River flow, solar irradiance, transit ridership, shelter occupancy — forecasting with Prophet, ARIMA, and LSTM networks. Backtesting pipelines with rolling-origin cross-validation.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### Statistical experimentation
A/B testing frameworks combining frequentist hypothesis testing, Bayesian posterior inference, and sequential analysis. Power calculations, sample size planning, and meta-analysis across experiment portfolios.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="50%" colspan="2" valign="top">

### AI consulting
Helping businesses identify where machine learning creates real value — and where it does not. Scoping ML use cases, auditing model readiness, building proof-of-concept pipelines, and translating technical results into stakeholder-ready recommendations. Focus on practical adoption over hype.

</td>
</tr>
</table>
