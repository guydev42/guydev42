01 / About

Building things that learn

I am a data scientist and ML engineer based in Calgary with a Master's degree in Mathematical Sciences from the University of Cape Town. I turn raw data into production-grade systems that drive real business decisions. My work spans customer analytics, statistical experimentation, geospatial analysis, and end-to-end machine learning pipelines across 15 different industry domains.

I build the full stack of a data science project: from exploratory analysis and feature engineering through model training and evaluation to deployment, monitoring, and documentation. I lead projects end-to-end and mentor junior team members, translating complex analytical findings into clear, persuasive business recommendations for both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

My approach favors rigor over hype. I care about experiment design, calibration curves, and whether a model actually outperforms a strong baseline. I thrive in agile, remote-first environments and enjoy creating data-driven business stories that connect model outputs to actionable strategy.
